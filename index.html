Who we are

Varna Dance Theater is a cultural organization based in Varna, Bulgaria, and operating in the field of contemporary dance. It was launched in the spring of 2018 by two Varna-born dancers and cultural managers who had returned home after spending years abroad. Through our work we aspire to make contemporary dance an integral part of the cultural landscape of the city. It is our desire to contribute to the development of the performing arts in Varna, while giving talented choreographers and dancers an opportunity for professional growth.

Varna Dance Theater’s debut performance The Notice Board took place on July, 14, 2018 at the stage of Varna Opera House. Subsequently the production was supported by the Ministry of Culture of Bulgaria and selected to participate in Theatres’ Night – Varna 2018. In May 2019 Varna Dance Theater traveled to Edirne, Turkey, to show the performance at the behest of Trakya University and as part of the cultural program of the Bulgarian Consulate.

In July 2019, Varna Dance Theater conducted the first edition of Dance Routes – a contemporary dance festival aimed at bringing international dance experience and practices to Varna with the aid of dance professionals whose artistic journey has somehow been connected to the city.

Our mission

We strive to contribute to the development of the art of contemporary dance in our city and country. We are committed to enriching the cultural life of Varna while giving talented dance artists and choreographers the chance to perform, create, develop professionally, and be part of an evolving creative team. We believe in sharing experience and successful art practices across borders, and consider international collaborations as an integral part of our strategy.

Our Goals

  • Create, produce, and perform contemporary dance performances.
  • Present Varna in the sphere of contemporary dance on a national and international level.
  • Build sustainable national and international partnerships, thus making ground for future collaborations.
  • Exchange experience by inviting international guests and making visits abroad.
  • Cultivate strong contemporary dance artists.
  • Discover talented choreographers from Bulgaria and abroad, produce and popularize their work.
  • Launch and participate in a diverse range of projects involving contemporary dance.
